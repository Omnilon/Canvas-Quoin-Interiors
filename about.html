About Us

Established in 2016, Canvas Quoin Interiors grew from a shared love of design and craftsmanship. Our name reflects our belief that every space is a blank canvas ready to be shaped, and every detail – like a quoin in architecture – provides both support and beauty.

We work with clients worldwide to create unique interiors that reflect personality, culture and purpose.
